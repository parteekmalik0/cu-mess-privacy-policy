Privacy Policy for Cu Mess

Last updated: Feb 25, 2026

Cu Mess respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled in the app.

1. Information We Collect

Cu Mess does not require users to create an account or provide personal information.
However, the app uses third-party services that may process limited data necessary for functionality and advertising purposes, including:
Device identifiers (such as Firebase Installation ID and Advertising ID)
App interaction data (such as ad impressions and ad clicks)

Approximate location (derived from IP address for advertising purposes)
Cu Mess does not collect sensitive personal information such as:
Name
Email address
Phone number
Precise location
Financial information
Contacts

2. How We Use Information
Information is used only to:
Display meal-related content from the database
Send push notifications
Display advertisements
Maintain and improve core app functionality

3. Third-Party Services
Cu Mess uses Google services, including:
Cloud Firestore – Cloud database for meal data
Firebase Cloud Messaging – Push notifications
Google AdMob – In-app advertisements
These services may collect and process data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

4. Data Security
All data transmitted between the app and Google servers is encrypted using secure HTTPS protocols.

5. Data Deletion
Cu Mess does not require user accounts.
Users may stop data collection by:
Uninstalling the app, or

Resetting their device advertising ID in device settings (for ads-related data)

For more details about how Google handles data, users may review Google’s Privacy Policy.
